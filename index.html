Cubicler

A Game About Doing the Best at Your Job

Assemble the best desk to accomplish your tasks. Become the best office worker!

About Game

Cubicler is a deck-building strategy game about productivity, synergy, and the modern digital workspace. The player's goal is to build their ultimate cubicle setup by collecting and combining cards that represent tools of office efficiency. From ergonomic chairs and mechanical keyboards to AI assistants and productivity apps, there are many ways to customize your desk. Cubicler explores how incremental improvements and smart combinations lead to exponential gains in productivity!
Created by Nick Barney, Collin Follett, Antonios Ibrakhim, Steven Lonsdale-Verbiest, and Brandon Morrow for the Fall 2025 Senior Capstone at Oakland University.
